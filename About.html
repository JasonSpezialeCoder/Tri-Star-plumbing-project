About Tri-Star Plumbing

est 2009

Our Story...

Welcome to Tristar Plumbing and Heating Inc.! With over 75 dedicated employees, we are proud to be a leading provider of top-notch plumbing, heating, and HVAC services in the Tri-state area.

At Tristar Plumbing and Heating Inc., we understand the importance of reliable and efficient plumbing, heating, and HVAC systems in your home or business. That's why our team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering exceptional quality workmanship and unparalleled customer service.

who are we...

Whether you need assistance with plumbing installations, repairs, or maintenance, our experienced technicians have the expertise to handle any challenge with precision and care. From fixing leaky faucets to installing state-of-the-art heating systems, we've got you covered.

When it comes to heating and HVAC services, we are well-equipped to tackle any project. Our team is proficient in installing, repairing, and maintaining various heating systems, ensuring optimal comfort and energy efficiency throughout the year. From furnaces and boilers to heat pumps and ductless mini-splits, we have the knowledge and skills to meet all your heating needs.

what we stand for...

At Tristar Plumbing and Heating Inc., we take pride in our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and innovative solutions. We prioritize customer satisfaction above all else, striving to exceed your expectations with every interaction. Our friendly and professional staff is here to assist you every step of the way, providing personalized recommendations and expert advice tailored to your unique needs.

Whether you're a homeowner, business owner, or property manager, you can trust Tristar Plumbing and Heating Inc. to deliver exceptional services that ensure your plumbing, heating, and HVAC systems are functioning optimally. Contact us today to experience the Tristar difference and discover why we are the go-to choice for all your plumbing and heating needs in the Tri-state area.
